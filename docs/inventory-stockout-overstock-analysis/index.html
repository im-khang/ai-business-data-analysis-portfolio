Triage rationale

Why these candidates appear

Candidates surface when a seller-state, customer-state, and category combination has meaningful late-order volume plus late-rate context. Use this queue to decide where to investigate first, not to assign fault.

Operations checks

  • Review delivery promise accuracy for highlighted lanes and categories.
  • Check seller fulfillment handoffs before changing operations policy.
  • Compare carrier lane or route patterns against missed SLA promises.
  • Validate with company-owned inventory snapshots before production stock decisions.
